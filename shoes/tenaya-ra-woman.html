Frequently Asked Questions

What climbing styles is the Tenaya Ra Woman designed for?

The Ra Woman is designed primarily for bouldering and bouldering and sport and trad/multi-pitch climbing. It performs particularly well on indoor, limestone.

Is the Tenaya Ra Woman suitable for beginners?

No. The Ra Woman has an aggressive downturn and asymmetric shape that requires precise footwork to use effectively. It is intended for intermediate to advanced climbers.

Does the Tenaya Ra Woman fit wide feet?

The Ra Woman is available in narrow width only. Climbers with wide feet may find it a poor fit and should try it on before buying.

Can the Tenaya Ra Woman be resoled?

Yes. The Ra Woman can be resoled, which significantly extends the shoe's lifespan and reduces long-term cost. Most climbing shoe repair specialists offer resoling from around £35–£55.

Is the Tenaya Ra Woman vegan?

Yes. The Ra Woman is made without any animal-derived materials and is suitable for vegan climbers.

How should I size the Tenaya Ra Woman?

Women's Ra; wider last, size down 0.5-1 EU from street shoe size.

What rock types is the Tenaya Ra Woman best suited to?

The Ra Woman performs best on indoor, limestone. Its rubber compound and profile are optimised for these surfaces.

Is the Tenaya Ra Woman a women's specific shoe?

Yes. The Ra Woman is a women's specific shoe with a last designed for a typically narrower heel and lower-volume foot shape.

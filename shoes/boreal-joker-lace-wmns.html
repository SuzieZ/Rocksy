Frequently Asked Questions

What climbing styles is the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS designed for?

The Joker Lace WMNS is designed primarily for sport and trad/multi-pitch climbing. It performs particularly well on indoor, granite, sandstone, gritstone, cracks.

Is the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS suitable for beginners?

Yes. The Joker Lace WMNS has a flat, relaxed profile — it prioritises comfort and is well-suited to beginners, gym climbers, and anyone spending long sessions on the wall.

Does the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS fit wide feet?

The Joker Lace WMNS is available in narrow width only. Climbers with wide feet may find it a poor fit and should try it on before buying.

What toe shape does the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS suit?

The Joker Lace WMNS is shaped for climbers with a centred foot shape. If your toes don't match this profile, you may find the fit uncomfortable at the toe box.

Can the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS be resoled?

Yes. The Joker Lace WMNS can be resoled, which significantly extends the shoe's lifespan and reduces long-term cost. Most climbing shoe repair specialists offer resoling from around £35–£55.

How should I size the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS?

What rock types is the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS best suited to?

Is the Boreal Joker Lace WMNS a women's specific shoe?

Yes. The Joker Lace WMNS is a women's specific shoe with a last designed for a typically narrower heel and lower-volume foot shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

What climbing styles is the Unparallel Engage VCS LV designed for?

The Engage VCS LV is designed primarily for bouldering and sport climbing. It performs particularly well on indoor, limestone, sandstone.

Is the Unparallel Engage VCS LV suitable for beginners?

The Engage VCS LV has a moderate profile that suits intermediate climbers. It is usable by confident beginners but will feel most natural to someone past their first year of climbing.

Does the Unparallel Engage VCS LV fit wide feet?

The Engage VCS LV is available in narrow width only. Climbers with wide feet may find it a poor fit and should try it on before buying.

What toe shape does the Unparallel Engage VCS LV suit?

The Engage VCS LV is shaped for climbers with a centred foot shape. If your toes don't match this profile, you may find the fit uncomfortable at the toe box.

Can the Unparallel Engage VCS LV be resoled?

Yes. The Engage VCS LV can be resoled, which significantly extends the shoe's lifespan and reduces long-term cost. Most climbing shoe repair specialists offer resoling from around £35–£55.

How should I size the Unparallel Engage VCS LV?

LV of Engage VCS; leather upper

What rock types is the Unparallel Engage VCS LV best suited to?

The Engage VCS LV performs best on indoor, limestone, sandstone. Its rubber compound and profile are optimised for these surfaces.

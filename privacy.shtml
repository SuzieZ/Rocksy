Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Rocksy Adventures ("we", "us", "our") runs the Climbing Shoe Finder at rocksyadventures.com. This page explains what data we collect, why, and what choices you have. We're a small business — we keep this simple, and we don't collect more than we need.

Who we are

Rocksy Adventures is the data controller for the personal data described below. If you have questions, use the Feedback button on the site to get in touch.

What we collect

DataWhenWhy
Name (optional)If you submit the feedback formSo we can credit or follow up with you, if you choose to share it
Feedback messageIf you submit the feedback formTo improve the quiz and our shoe recommendations
Star ratingIf you submit the feedback formTo gauge how well the tool is working
Quiz answers (experience, style, foot width, budget, etc.)While using the quizTo generate your shoe recommendations — processed in your browser, not stored by us
Usage data (pages viewed, button clicks, approximate location/device)Automatically, via Google AnalyticsTo understand how the tool is used and improve it
Outbound link clicks ("Buy now")When you click through to a retailerTo understand which shoes are popular and measure referral activity

Quiz answers aren't stored

Your answers to the shoe-finder questions stay in your browser to generate results. We don't save your quiz answers on our servers.

Feedback form

If you use the Feedback button, your name (if given), message, and rating are sent via Formspree, a third-party form processing service, to our inbox. Formspree processes this data on our behalf as a data processor. We don't sell or share this information with anyone else. We periodically delete old feedback submissions we no longer need.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how people use the quiz — for example, which questions cause drop-off, and which "Buy now" links get clicked. This involves cookies or similar identifiers set by Google. Analytics data is aggregated and not used to identify you personally by us.

Outbound retailer links

"Buy now" buttons take you to third-party retailers (such as Banana Fingers, La Sportiva, Scarpa, Adidas/Five Ten, or Evolv). These links may include tracking parameters (UTM tags, and in future, affiliate identifiers) so we can see which products are popular and, where applicable, earn a commission. Once you leave our site, the retailer's own privacy policy applies — we have no control over their data practices.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics to recognise repeat visits and measure usage. We don't currently use cookies for advertising or to identify you personally.

Legal basis for processing

Where UK/EU GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Consent — for feedback you choose to submit, including your name if provided.
  • Legitimate interests — for analytics and outbound click tracking, to understand and improve the tool, balanced against your privacy.

How long we keep data

Feedback submissions are kept only as long as useful for improving the site, and are deleted periodically. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard retention settings.

Your rights

If UK/EU data protection law applies to you, you have the right to ask us to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you (such as a feedback submission), and to object to or restrict certain processing. To make a request, use the Feedback button to contact us with enough detail (e.g. your name and approximate submission date) for us to find your entry.

Third parties we use

  • Formspree — processes feedback form submissions
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics
  • GoDaddy — website hosting

Changes to this policy

We may update this page as the site evolves — for example, when we add affiliate tracking. We'll update the "last updated" date above when we do.
